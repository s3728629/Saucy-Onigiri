Beyong Current

Capabilities

The idea of the project is to extend your individual current IT knowledge. In this section you will explain what your current knowledge is and how completion of the project will extend it. In particular, we are looking to see how someone who might consider coding to not be their best skill, to use this project to improve their coding skills. If you are honest, this project will be of great benefit to your IT development. It’s important that each group member tries to move out of their current “comfort zone”.

All members in our group are first year IT students, meaning that our skills aren’t as refined as the industry standard. However, we each have our strengths and weaknesses that balance out our capabilities in the team. A number of our team members, - Jessani, Jade, Akeela, Yumi and Anisah - are strong in design and ux related areas and weaker at web programming with the exception of Jade and Jessani who are comfortable with coding in HTML. Dylan’s strength lies in Github, web programming and networking and his area to develop is design. Therefore pairing him with a more design oriented team member with weaker programming skills ensure that both can support each other and develop their skills. We have 3 strong web programmers and 3 strong designers, so pairing up will work well. We took all of this into consideration when assigning the roles, and ensured that everyone gets a chance to develop their skills in an area they feel needs work on. Since our project is a website, it's a perfect opportunity to develop our HTML coding and UX design skills to ensure our website is as smooth running and simple to use as possible.

Project Risks

Since Australia is such a diverse country, it will be a challenge to translate into every single language spoken here for each of our videos’ closed captions so we will likely use google translate or other reliable auto-translate technologies to make it as accessible as possible. However, we could always push that back and add translation as an update later on if it takes too long to develop and will delay the launch of our product. Another challenge will be the AI chatbot. As a team, we do not have the necessary skills and experience in developing such a complex feature so we will need to work on developing our skills for it. Having such an intricate feature, it’s vital that our website can support it without crashing or causing any errors. During the project, we will test it, however, there can always be errors we did not pick up on. Another possible challenge will be the quiz. We assigned two strong web programmers to take charge in developing that feature as it is something none of us have much experience with. We may not be able to complete a comprehensive enough quiz that covers the content with a simple interface. Additionally, coding a quiz that can correct answers and suggest a relevant point of reference for an incorrect answer will be a challenge so we aim to do as much research and consult industry persons with experience in this area that can guide us.
